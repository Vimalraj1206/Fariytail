Beauty and the Beast




Beauty along with her siblings and father had once been rich but as their house caught on fire they lost everything. Their father in search of a new beginning for them went off on a journey. As he was leaving beauty told him that she wanted him to bring her back a rose. Having failed his mission and not being able to recovered what they had he was strolling back home when he found a castle and decided to explore in search of someone who could help him.

When the beast asked her to marry him she said yes. Soon after her response he turned into a charming prince! A fairy and the queen came out to thank her for saving the prince from the terrible enchantment. The queen granted her consent for the weeding and so beauty and the prince lived happily ever after.
